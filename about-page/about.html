John Waugh

Appalachian State 26' B.S. Computer Science

As a Computer Science student at Appalachian State University, I am passionate about learning and applying new technologies and skills to solve real-world problems. I have completed multiple online courses and certifications in Python and HTML5, which I use to develop and improve web applications and embedded systems.

Currently, I am an Embedded Systems Engineer and a Race Operations Sub Lead at Team Sunergy, the university's solar vehicle team. In this role, I work with a multidisciplinary team of students and faculty to design, build, and race solar-powered cars. I am responsible for debugging and enhancing the telemetry systems that monitor and display the car's data, as well as representing the team at university events, brainstorming and planning fundraising and sponsorship strategies, and recruiting new team members through social media and advertisements.

I have also assumed the role of Composites Specialist at Team Sunergy, which includes the design and fabrication of structurally strong and lightweight components made from composite materials (fiberglass, kevlar, flax, carbon fiber), and also the utilization of techniques such as vacuum bagging, resin infusion, and wet layup molding/compression molding.

Previously, I was a Sales Associate at Play It Again Sports, where I gained valuable experience in sales, customer relations, and equipment delivery logistics.

I am looking for internship opportunities in various computer science-related fields, such as embedded systems, full-stack web design, and web app development. I am eager to learn from and contribute to innovative and impactful projects.
